Our Story

A historic building full of stories, waiting for yours

Named after Guy — the charming, slightly obsessive treasure-hunter who runs the show — The Vintage Guy is unlike any shop you've walked into before.

We're nestled inside a stunning centuries-old stone building on the North Coast of Northern Ireland, just a stone's throw from the Giant's Causeway, Bushmills Distillery, and the Dark Hedges. Multiple dealers under one ancient roof means every single visit turns up something different.

Pine furniture with a past. Brass candlesticks that have lit a hundred dinners. Kilim cushions from a souk in Marrakech. Copper kettles. Vintage signs. Oil paintings of stormy seas. We source across Europe so your home can have a piece of everywhere.

Stock moves fast. If something catches your eye — call us. We hold for 24 hours.

What We Stock

Everything old
is new again.

From grand antique furniture to tiny curios you'll wonder how you ever lived without — the place is packed. And it changes every week.

Furniture & Cabinets
Antique pine, dark wood dressers, Georgian chests, farmhouse tables. Pieces with character and a lifetime left in them.
Ceramics & Kitchenware
Stoneware crocks, vintage pottery, copper kettles, enamelware. The kind of stuff that makes a kitchen feel lived-in and loved.
Art & Prints
Original oil paintings, vintage maps, botanical prints, seascape watercolours. Something for every wall.
Textiles & Rugs
Kilim cushions, vintage sari throws, Persian-style rugs, colourful patchwork. Add warmth, texture, and a story to any room.
Brass, Copper & Silver
Candlesticks, barley-twist torchere stands, copper kettles, silver-plate. The accent pieces that make a home feel complete.
Curiosities & Gifts
Vintage signs, prop-worthy oddities, collectible tins, rare books, and genuinely unusual gifts. If you need something no one else will have — start here.
What We Do

More than a shop.
Much more.

Guy and the team go above and beyond for anyone who walks through the door. Here's how we can help:

01
Sourcing & Hunting
Can't find the perfect piece? We'll find it for you. Regular sourcing trips across Europe mean we can track down almost anything. Just describe what you're after and we'll do the hunting.
02
Home Staging
Selling your home? Our home staging service uses curated vintage and antique pieces to make any space feel warm, lived-in, and genuinely desirable. Ask us about availability.
03
House & Business Clearances
We handle clearances with care and respect. Everything goes to good homes, not landfill. Fair valuations. Hassle-free. We take the hard work off your hands.
04
Film & Theatre Props
We've supplied props for movies and productions already — and we'd love to work on yours. From period-accurate furniture to quirky one-offs, this historic space is a filmmaker's dream.
05
Event & Wedding Hire
Need to hire or buy pieces for a wedding, pop-up, or event? We can dress your venue in genuine vintage character that no catalogue supplier can match.
06
Delivery & Shipping
Free local delivery. Worldwide shipping arranged at cost. Your new favourite thing will make it home — wherever that is.
